# Food Hygiene Rating App
## About this Application
The Food Hygiene Rating App helps users to find and review food hygiene ratings for businesses in Bristol, United Kingdom. It allows consumers to search businesses by Business Name, Location or hygiene ratings. The application will include Geolocation support, filtering options and forms for users to report food safety issues and to request rating corrections to business owners. The introduction of this app will help people be more aware of the hygiene standards of businesses near them and encourages business owners to promote food safety.
